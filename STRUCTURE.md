# SEED Structure

SEED can be understood as a simple conceptual structure.

Core:

SEED
│
├ Self Observation
├ Perspective Expansion
├ Cognitive Exploration
└ Structural Awareness


Process:

1. Know thyself
2. Observe
3. Recognize patterns
4. Expand perspective
5. Continue the journey


SEED is not a doctrine.

It is an open structure for thought and exploration.
